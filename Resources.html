Mental Health Apps (Subscription Required)

Meditate, mindfully, with the Headspace app. A collection of themed meditations to benefit your emotional wellbeing and health. Click here for the Headspace app

AI Therapist apps to guide you in conversations 24/7. If you're waiting for your next appointment or seeking some guidance, AI therapy apps are available to guide. Please note that they are not to replace conventional therapy and/or psychiatry and medical help. There are many apps available in the marketplaces, to name some, Wysa and Yuna.

Google Gemini and ChatGPT are two major AI-backed chatbots that can essentially guide you and help curate ideas and moments of realizations. Note, they are both free; there are subscription versions with different features. Please note, they are not meant to replace conventional therapy and/or psychiatry and medical help.

Learn about emotional resilience and psychology for enhancing your emotional wellbeing.
