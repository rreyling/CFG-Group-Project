Breathe Easy with Guided Affirmations

Guided affirmations are a great way to encourage intent to visualize and alchemize positive effect and change through using affirmations, statements that encourage you to think in ways that affirm your goals and lifestyle. Affirmations can range from a variety of themes, including health, wellness, relationships, self-esteem, confidence, self-worth, aesthetic, finance, career, and many more. There are a lot of guided affirmations that can be played over streaming services such as YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, and more.

Here are some channels on YouTube that are great for starting the day, refreshing, reseting, and giving a healthy, natural confidence boost for the day. Even if your day doesn't start as hoped for or intended, it's never too late to listen and watch one of these visualization videos and bring it brighter and beautiful! They offer great affirmations and beautiful nature scenery for visualization. Enjoy!

Affirmations by Lavendaire

Lavendaire is a YouTuber who has created gorgeous, gorgeous affirmation videos and journals for everyone to become inspired and their best selves. Enjoy beautiful visualization and keep calm and cool with Lavendaire as you live your best life!

Affirmations by Leeor Alexandra

Discover affirmations and affirm self-love, confidence, worth, and much more with Leeor Alexandra. Manifest, visualize, and boost your quality of life and happiness with her visualization-affirmation videos on YouTube.

Affirmations by Alanna Foxx

Explore affirmations, beauty, motherhood, home living, design, and more with Alanna Foxx on YouTube. Start your day fresh or boost yourself with affirmations to feel amazing.
