Healthy Teas, Fruits, and Veggies

Teas are a refreshing way to get hydrated and healthy. Some teas that may help aid in breathing and may clear breathing pathways are: peppermint tea, green tea, licorice root tea, thyme tea, ginger tea, mullein tea, eucalyptus tea, to name several. Teas have multiple benefits to them, so stay refreshed and improve your general health, while you breathe easy!

Drinking warm water can help with breathing easy! Warm water with honey, lemon, peppermint, turmeric, and ginger, to name several options to consider while drinking warm water, are healthy for lung improvement.

Milk and flavors can be a comfortable & delicious way to breathe easy! Spiced and flavored milks, such as turmeric milk, cinnamon milk, honey milk, cardamom milk, and ginger milk, are some great milk drinks to consider in the aid of lung clearing, respiratory health, and breathing easy.

Fruit and vegetable juices are refreshing ways to stay hydrated and healthy. Breathe easy with beet juice, carrot juice, apple juice, citrus juices, pineapple juice, berry juices, tomato juice, and watermelon juice. Try juicing these fruits and vegetables for freshly-made juices with lesser sugar intake.
