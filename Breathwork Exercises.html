Breathing Techniques

  • Relax breathe bubble
  • Balance
    Inhale 4 seconds - Hold 2 - Exhale 4 seconds
    "Inhale through nose-to-belly to relax the body and feel more present."
  • Restore
    Inhale 5 seconds - Exhale 5 seconds
    "Inhale through your belly to strengthen your body and restore energy."
  • Focus
    Inhale 4 seconds - Hold 4 - Exhale 4 seconds - Hold 4
    With this box breathing technique, let yourself keep cool, calm, and in concentration.
  • Energize
    Inhale 4 seconds - Exhale 2 seconds
    "Boost your energy."
  • Unwind
    Inhale 4 seconds - Hold 7 - Exhale 8 seconds
    "Release tension."

Benefits of Intentional Breathing

  1. Reduces stress and anxiety
  2. Lowers blood pressure
  3. Improves sleep
  4. Increases focus and concentration
  5. Manages pain
